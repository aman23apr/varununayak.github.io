About Me.


I am a second year Masters student in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Stanford University, where I am specializing in Robotics and Artificial Intelligence.

Recently, I completed a summer internship at Auris Health, Inc., a surgical robotics company owned by Johnson and Johnson.

I completed my Bachelors Degree in Mechanical Engineering from Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani, India. In my final year, I pursued a thesis under Dr. Kostas Alexis at University of Nevada, Reno. I also spent a few months performing research at Bhabha Atomic Research Center, a government research facility in Mumbai, India.
